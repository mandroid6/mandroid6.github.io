## About Me

I am Mandar Deshpande, a Model Optimization Engineer at Roblox. Previously a Systems ML Engineer at Meta, where I optimized GPU kernels and integrated/developed Triton with PyTorch for NVIDIA/AMD hardware, specializing in PyTorch-based preprocessing transforms and model serialization for large-scale recommendation system models.

My work spans deep learning, GPU optimization (Triton, CUDA), and large-scale data processing.
